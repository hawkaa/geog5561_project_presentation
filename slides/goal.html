Project goal

Maximize profit and minimize risk in the process of finding new customers for Internet Service Providers (ISP)

The project's goal is to use a geographical information system to maximize profit and minimize risk in the process of finding new customers for an internet service provider.

By an internet service provider I mean a company that supplies high speed broadband connections to households and businesses, the companies responsible for connecting their cables to your house so you can enjoy netflix, spotify and facebook.
